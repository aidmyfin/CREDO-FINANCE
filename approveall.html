To proceed with your application under our Low Credit Acceptance Program, a once-off R269 fee is required.

Why it matters:

This process gives you a real opportunity to secure a loan and start improving your financial standing, as responsible repayment can positively support your credit profile over time.

  • The fee Does not reduce your principal loan amount.
  • The fee must be paid separately to PERFECT FINANCE to initiate the disbursement protocol.

IMPORTANT: The one-time refundable Low Credit Service Fee of R269.00 must be settled to finalize your application.
