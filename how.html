What You Need

Basic Requirements to Apply

We keep our requirements minimal. Most South Africans qualify — even those with less-than-perfect credit.

Valid South African ID

A green barcoded ID book or Smart ID card is required. Foreign nationals must provide a valid passport and work permit.

Proof of Income

Last 3 months' payslips or bank statements showing regular income. Self-employed applicants may provide financial statements.

Proof of Residence

A recent utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement not older than 3 months showing your current address.

Active Bank Account

A valid South African bank account in your name where funds will be deposited and debit orders will be processed.

Age 18 or Older

You must be at least 18 years old to apply for a loan with Credo Finance, in accordance with South African law.

Valid Contact Details

An active South African phone number and email address to receive updates, verification codes, and loan agreements.
